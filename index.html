How to set up your CF Worker Proxy
  1. Go to workers.cloudflare.com and create a free account.
  2. Click Create Worker → paste the contents of worker.js (included in the zip).
  3. Click Save & Deploy — you'll get a URL like my-proxy.workers.dev.
  4. Copy that URL and paste it into the Proxy URL field above, then hit SAVE.
  5. Done! The browser will now route requests through your personal worker. 🐧
