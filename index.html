What is a Proxy?

FrostOS routes your browser traffic through a proxy so you can access sites freely. By default we use a public CORS proxy — it works out of the box but may be slower or blocked on some networks.

BYOP — Bring Your Own Proxy

For the best experience, deploy your own proxy backend. This gives you faster speeds and more reliability. Three options:

Ultraviolet Full web proxy. Deploy on Cloudflare Workers or any Node host. Best compatibility.
Rammerhead Session-based proxy. Great for sites that block standard proxies.
CORS Proxy Simplest setup. Deploy corsproxy.io or similar on any server.
How to Set It Up
1Deploy your proxy backend (Ultraviolet / Rammerhead / CORS)
2Open Settings → Proxy Engine in FrostOS
3Select your engine and paste your backend URL
4Browse freely through the Browser tab 🎉
